# Quick Facts

### Quick Facts

PASSPORT VALIDITY:

Valid at time of entry

BLANK PASSPORT PAGES:

One page required.

TOURIST VISA REQUIRED:

Not required for stays under 180 days

VACCINATIONS:

None

CURRENCY RESTRICTIONS FOR ENTRY:

You must declare $10,000 CAN or more.

CURRENCY RESTRICTIONS FOR EXIT:

You must declare $10,000 CAN or more.
