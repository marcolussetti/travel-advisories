# Natural disasters and climate

## Natural disasters and climate

### Climate change

Climate change is affecting the Central African Republic. Extreme and unusual weather events are becoming more frequent and may affect your travel plans. Monitor local media for the latest information.

### Rainy season

The rainy season extends from May to October. As you head north in the country, the length of the rainy season gradually decreases to four months, from June to September.
