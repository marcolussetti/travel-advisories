### IRAQ - AVOID ALL TRAVEL

[Avoid all travel](#levels "Risk Levels") to Iraq due to ongoing tensions in the region, and the unpredictable and dangerous security situation.

### Southern and Central Governorates - Avoid non-essential travel

[Avoid non-essential travel](#levels "Risk Levels") to the following southern and central governorates due to violence between rival groups, kidnappings, and the volatile security situation:

* Baghdad
* Wasit
* Maysan
* Basra, including:
  + The city of Basra,
  + the port city of Al Faw and the main road leading there
  + Highway 8, between Basra and the border with Kuwait
* Dhi Qar
* Qadisiyyah
* Babil
* Karbala
* beyond 100 km from the border with Saudi Arabia in the Governorates of Al Najaf and Al Muthanna

This advisory excludes the following areas where you should avoid all travel:

* Sadr City in Baghdad Governorate
* Jufr al-Shakhar in Babil Governorate
* areas within 30 km of the borders with Iran, Kuwait and Saudi Arabia

### Kurdistan Region of Iraq - Avoid non-essential travel

[Avoid non-essential travel](#levels "Risk Levels") to the following areas of the Kurdistan Region of Iraq due to the volatile security situation, unexploded ordnance, and the threat of terrorism:

* Erbil, excluding the district of Makhmur
* Sulaymaniyah
* Duhok
  + The city of Halabja and route 180 leading there
  + Shwan and Qarah Hanjir/Ar-Rabi’ subdistricts in the Kirkuk Governorate
  + Akre, Shekhan, Al Shikhan districts and Kalak subdistrict in the Nineveh Governorate

This advisory excludes areas within 20 km of the borders with Türkiye and Iran where you should avoid all travel.
