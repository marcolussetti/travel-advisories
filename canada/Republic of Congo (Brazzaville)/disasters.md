# Natural disasters and climate

## Natural disasters and climate

### Climate change

Climate change is affecting the Republic of the Congo. Extreme and unusual weather events are becoming more frequent and may affect your travel plans. Monitor local media for the latest information.

### Rainy seasons

The rainy seasons extend from:

* September to December, then from March to May in the southern part of the country, which includes Brazzaville and Pointe-Noire
* April to October in the north

Seasonal flooding can hamper overland travel and reduce the provision of essential services. Roads may become impassable and infrastructure may be damaged.

* Monitor local media for the latest information, including on road conditions
* Stay away from flooded areas
* Monitor weather reports
* Follow the instructions of local authorities, including evacuation orders
