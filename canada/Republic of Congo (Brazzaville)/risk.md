### Republic of the Congo (Brazzaville) - Exercise a high degree of caution

[Exercise a high degree of caution](#levels "Risk Levels") in the Republic of the Congo due to crime and insecurity in several neighbouring countries.

### Border area with the Central African Republic - Avoid non-essential travel

[Avoid non-essential travel](#levels "Risk Levels") to within 50 km of the border with the Central African Republic due to the presence of criminal gangs and armed militias.
