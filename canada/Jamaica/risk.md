### Jamaica - Exercise a high degree of caution

[Exercise a high degree of caution](#levels "Risk Levels") in Jamaica due to the high level of violent crime, particularly outside tourist areas.

### Western parishes - Avoid non-essential travel

[Avoid non-essential travel](#levels "Risk Levels") to in the following parishes due to extensive damage caused by Hurricane Melissa:

* St Catherine
* Clarendon
* Manchester
* St Elizabeth, excluding Treasure Beach and the T1 to A2 roads leading there from Kingston
* Westmoreland, excluding Negril and the A1 road leading there from Ocho Rios/Montego Bay
* Hanover, excluding Lucea and the A1 road
* St James, excluding Montego Bay and the coastal area along the A1 road
