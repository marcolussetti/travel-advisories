## Natural disasters and climate

### Earthquakes

Azerbaijan is located in an active seismic zone.

[Seismic alerts](https://seismology.az/en) – Republican Seismic Survey Center of the Azerbaijan National Academy of Sciences

### Flooding and landslides

Flooding may occur in parts of Azerbaijan, especially on the Southern Caspian coast in the Lankaran-Astara region. Heavy rains can overwhelm drainage systems in urban centres, including Baku, and increase the risk of flash flooding. They can hamper overland travel and reduce the provision of essential services. Landslides could occur, making roads impassable and damaging infrastructure.

* Monitor local media for the latest information, including on road conditions
* Stay away from flooded areas
* Monitor weather reports
* Follow the instructions of local authorities, including evacuation orders
