## Entry requirements

### **Visa**

Irish citizens do not require a visa to enter the Cayman Islands**.**

If you are unsure of the entry requirements for this country, including visa and other immigration information, ask your travel agent or contact the country's nearest [**Embassy or Consulate**](/en/dfa/embassies-in-ireland/).

### **Passport**

A valid passport is required for travel to the Cayman Islands. Irish passports should be valid beyond the date of their intended departure and onward tickets are required for all visitors. Passport cards cannot be used.
