## Natural disasters and climate

### **Flooding**

During the rainy season from June to September, torrential rain can cause flooding and landslides including on main roads in Banjul.

### **Harmattan**

From November to April, The Gambia is affected by the Harmattan wind, a dry and dusty wind from the Sahara desert. The Harmattan can reduce visibility and air quality, and may cause respiratory issues. Travellers are advised to check the air quality during this period and adjust their plans accordingly – this may mean wearing facemasks or avoiding outdoor activities.
