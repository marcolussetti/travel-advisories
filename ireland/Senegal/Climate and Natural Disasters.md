## Climate and Natural Disasters

### **Flooding**

During the rainy season from June to September, torrential rain can cause flooding and landslides including on main roads in Dakar.

### **Harmattan**

From November to April, Senegal is affected by the Harmattan wind, a dry and dusty wind from the Sahara desert. The Harmattan can reduce visibility and cause respiratory issues.
