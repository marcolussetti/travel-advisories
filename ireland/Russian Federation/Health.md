## Health

**Travel Insurance**

Irish citizens should be aware that we cannot pay for emergency medical repatriation, repatriation of remains, or for expenses as a result of a personal emergency while you are abroad. If you buy an appropriate travel insurance policy, these costs will be covered, provided you haven’t broken the terms and conditions. Please note that it may be more difficult to find comprehensive travel insurance for Russia following the illegal full-scale invasion of Ukraine in 2022.

**Water**

Tap water is not drinkable throughout the Russian Federation, but bottled mineral water is widely available.
