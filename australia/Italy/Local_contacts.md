# Local contacts

#### Emergencies

Depending on what you need, contact your:

* family and friends
* travel agent
* insurance provider
* employer
* airline

#### Fire and rescue services

Call 112 or 115.

#### Medical emergencies

Call 112 or 118.

#### Police

Call 112 or 113.

#### European Emergency number

Call 112.

Always get a police report when you report a crime.

Your insurer should have a 24-hour emergency number.
