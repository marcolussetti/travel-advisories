# Local contacts

#### Emergencies

Depending on what you need, contact your:

* family and friends
* travel agent
* insurance provider
* employer
* airline

#### Fire and rescue services

Call 118.

#### Medical emergencies

Call 69, or go to the hospital.

#### Police

Call 117, or go to the nearest police station.

Always get a police report when you report a crime.

Your insurer should have a 24-hour emergency number.

#### Consular contacts

Read the [Consular Services Charter](/node/46) for what the Australian Government can and can't do to help you overseas.

Australia doesn't have an embassy in Benin.

You can access limited consular services from the Australian High Commission in Abuja, Nigeria.

In a consular emergency, if you can't contact an embassy, call the 24-hour Consular Emergency Centre on:

* +61 2 6261 3305 from overseas
* 1300 555 135 in Australia
